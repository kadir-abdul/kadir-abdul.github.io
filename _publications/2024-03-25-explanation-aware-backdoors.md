We study adversaries that keep classification accuracy intact while manipulating Grad-CAM explanations to mislead users. Our defense examines weight-shift statistics to detect tampering and introduces a post-hoc correction step that reduces attack success by ~99% without retraining.

Read the full methodology on [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.16569).

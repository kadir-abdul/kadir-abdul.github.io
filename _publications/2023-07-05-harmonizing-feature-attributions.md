This work evaluates whether feature attribution techniques can act as architecture-agnostic detectors of salient regions. We compare multiple explanation methods on CNNs and vision transformers, highlight inconsistencies, and introduce harmonization steps that stabilize explanations across models.

Read the LNCS chapter or the open preprint via [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.02150).
